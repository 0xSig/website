What is Arrow?

Arrow is building open source vertical-takeoff aircraft and a rideshare protocol that will bring point to point air travel to everyone.

We are growing a global community of dreamers who believe that air travel is ripe for disruption, and we can all do it together. Our core mission is to increase human physical connectedness by building infrastructure for on-demand, rapid travel between any two points on Earth.
